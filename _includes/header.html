Home of Marc-Andre Bazergui's LEGO® adventures


LEGO® MINDSTORMS® Community Partner & Ambassador of Fun, Marc-Andre is also part of the LEGO MINDSTORMS Expert Panel who helped provide design input that led to the creation of the EV3 retail set. Bazmarc, as he is known online, is famous for creating look-alikes of famous pop-culture robots such as R2-D2 and WALL-E. He is also admin and moderator of the MINDSTORMS EV3 Facebook group with over 10,000 members.
