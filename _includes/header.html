Home of Marc-Andre Bazergui's LEGO® adventures


Founder of the ROBOTMAK3RS Recognized LEGO® Online Community (RLOC) Formerly the MINDSTORMS Community Partner Marc-Andre was also part of the LEGO MINDSTORMS Expert Panel who helped provide design input that led to the creation of the EV3 retail set. Bazmarc, as he is known online, is famous for creating look-alikes of famous pop-culture robots such as R2-D2 and WALL-E. He has traveled the world with his robots and has participated in and helps organize robot experts zone in many LEGO related events such as WRO, FLL, MakerFaire and LEGO WORLD. Today he admin and moderator of all of the ROBOTMAK3RS Facebook groups, MINDSTORMS 51515, EV3, Spike Prime, WeDo, Boost and PoweredUP
